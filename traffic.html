Overview of Srikakulam Police Department

Welcome to the Srikakulam Police Department. We are committed to serving and protecting our community with integrity, professionalism, and dedication.

Traffic Rules and Regulations

  • Speed Limits: Follow posted speed limits strictly
  • Seat Belts: Mandatory for all occupants
  • Mobile Phones: No use while driving
  • Lane Discipline: Stay in your designated lane
  • Indicators: Use them for all turns

Penalties for Traffic Violations

Violation Penalty Amount
Over Speeding ₹1,000 - ₹2,000
Signal Jump ₹1,000 - ₹5,000
No Helmet ₹1,000
Drunk Driving ₹10,000

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I report a crime?
    Visit the nearest police station or call our emergency number.
  • How can I pay traffic fines?
    You can pay online through our website or visit the traffic police office.
  • What documents should I carry while driving?
    License, RC, Insurance, and PUC certificate.
