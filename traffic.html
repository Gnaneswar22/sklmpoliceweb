Sikakulam Police Department

Overview of Srikakulam Police Department

The Srikakulam Police Department is dedicated to safeguarding citizens, maintaining law and order, and upholding justice in the Srikakulam district. Our officers work tirelessly to prevent crime, ensure road safety, and assist the community. We emphasize community engagement and proactive policing to create a safe environment for everyone.

Traffic Rules and Regulations

General Driving Rules

  • Obey Speed Limits: Follow the posted speed limits to avoid accidents.
  • Seat Belts: Always wear seat belts while driving or as a passenger.
  • No Mobile Phones: Avoid using mobile phones while driving; use hands-free devices if necessary.
  • Follow Lane Discipline: Stick to your lane and avoid sudden lane changes.
  • Use Indicators: Always use indicators when turning or changing lanes.

Helmet and Seatbelt Requirements

It is mandatory for all two-wheeler riders to wear helmets. Car drivers and passengers must wear seat belts. Violations may lead to fines or penalties, as well as increased risk of injury.

Drink and Drive Policy

Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs is strictly prohibited. Random breathalyzer checks are conducted, and offenders face heavy fines, license suspension, or imprisonment.

Penalties for Traffic Violations

To maintain road safety, specific fines and penalties are imposed for various violations. Here’s an overview:

Violation Penalty
Overspeeding ₹1000 - ₹2000
Driving Without License ₹5000
Drunk Driving ₹10,000 or imprisonment up to 6 months
Not Wearing Seatbelt ₹1000
Using Mobile Phone ₹1000 - ₹5000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the documents I need to carry while driving?

Drivers must carry their driving license, vehicle registration certificate, insurance, and pollution under control (PUC) certificate.

2. How can I pay a traffic fine?

Traffic fines can be paid online through the official Srikakulam Police Department portal or at designated traffic police stations.

3. What should I do if I witness an accident?

If you witness an accident, report it immediately by calling the police helpline number. Provide assistance if possible without compromising your safety.
