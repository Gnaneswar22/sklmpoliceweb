Law & Order

The Srikakulam Police Department works tirelessly to uphold law and order in the community. Here is a detailed look at some of the major laws we enforce:

Criminal Law

Ensures that individuals committing criminal offenses face the law. Common areas covered:

  • Indian Penal Code (IPC): Addresses crimes like theft, assault, fraud, and others.
  • Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC): Governs criminal investigations, arrests, and court proceedings.
  • Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act: Controls the use and distribution of illegal substances.

Traffic Laws

Aimed at ensuring safe and responsible use of roads by all citizens:

  • Motor Vehicles Act: Regulates vehicle registration, licensing, and traffic offenses.
  • Helmet & Seatbelt Regulations: Mandatory for drivers and passengers for safety.
  • Drunk Driving Laws: Strict penalties for operating vehicles under the influence.

Cybercrime Laws

Handles cyber-related offenses to protect citizens from online crimes:

  • Information Technology Act: Covers cybercrimes like hacking, identity theft, and cyberbullying.
  • Data Protection Laws: Protects citizensâ€™ personal information and privacy online.

Public Safety Laws

Designed to protect public peace and order:

  • Arms Act: Regulates the possession and use of firearms and weapons.
  • Public Order Act: Addresses public gatherings and disturbances to ensure public peace.
  • Domestic Violence Act: Offers protection against violence in domestic settings.
