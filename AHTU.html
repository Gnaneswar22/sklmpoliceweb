Tracing Missing Cases

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to locate missing individuals, utilizing investigative skills, community collaboration, and advanced technology. We encourage anyone with information regarding a missing person to come forward and assist in our efforts. Note: All information shared remains confidential.

Awareness Programs on Good Touch & Bad Touch

We educate children on the importance of safe touch, offering tools to identify and report unsafe situations. Our programs empower children to protect themselves and foster a safe environment. Join us in spreading awareness by attending or hosting a workshop.

Rescue and Rehabilitation of Vulnerable Children

We rescue children in unsafe conditions, such as child labor and begging. Collaborating with welfare organizations, we provide shelter, education, and medical care. Help us give each child a safe, nurturing environment to grow and thrive.

Prevention of Child Marriage

Our initiative raises awareness about the harmful effects of child marriage, working closely with communities to protect children's rights. We aim to ensure every child enjoys a childhood free from undue responsibilities and has the chance to pursue education.
