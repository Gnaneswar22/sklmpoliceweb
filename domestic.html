Domestic Violence Support & Resources

Immediate help and support for those affected by domestic violence

Emergency Helpline: 100 | Women's Helpline: 181

Filing a Complaint

  1. Visit the nearest police station or call emergency services
  2. File a First Information Report (FIR)
  3. Request immediate protection if needed
  4. Connect with a Protection Officer

Legal Protections

  • Protection Orders

    Restraining orders against abusers

  • Residence Orders

    Secure your right to stay in shared household

  • Monetary Relief

    Compensation for expenses and losses

  • Custody Orders

    Protection of children's interests

Support Services

  • Legal Aid Services

    Free legal assistance and guidance

  • Medical Services

    Emergency medical care and documentation

  • Counseling Services

    Professional psychological support

  • Shelter Homes

    Safe accommodation for victims

Important Laws

  • Section 498A IPC: Cruelty by husband or relatives
  • Section 506 IPC: Criminal intimidation
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

Documentation Checklist

  • Medical records and reports
  • Police complaints and FIR copies
  • Photographs of injuries or damage
  • Written accounts of incidents
  • Witness statements
